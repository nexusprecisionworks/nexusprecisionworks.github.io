Quality Control Process

Comprehensive quality assurance at every stage

1

Incoming Material Inspection

All raw materials are thoroughly inspected for composition, dimensions, and quality before being accepted for production.

  • Material composition verification
  • Dimensional accuracy check
  • Surface quality assessment
2

In-Process Quality Control

Continuous monitoring and inspection during manufacturing to ensure quality standards are maintained throughout production.

  • Real-time dimensional monitoring
  • Surface finish verification
  • Process parameter validation
3

Final Inspection

Comprehensive final inspection using advanced measurement equipment to verify all specifications are met.

  • 100% dimensional inspection
  • Surface finish measurement
  • Material hardness testing
4

Quality Documentation

Complete documentation of all quality inspections and test results for traceability and compliance.

  • Inspection reports generation
  • Certificate of compliance
  • Quality records maintenance
