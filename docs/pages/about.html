About Nexus Precision Works

Your Trusted Partner in Precision Manufacturing

Our Story

Nexus Precision Works is a proprietorship firm established by Mr. Vaibhav Pandharinath Khalane with the main objective of contributing towards the Manufacturing, Automobile & Allied Industries in Indian markets and International markets.

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as a manufacturer of precision machine components for automobile and allied industries. We have a dedicated team for our production activities including engineers & skilled laborers. We have a well-equipped machine shop with all necessary tooling and measuring systems, along with other quality vendors working with us. We also provide milling, grinding & thread rolling facility by outsourcing.

Under a short span of less than 6 months, the entity understood the importance of customer satisfaction and conducted a survey of the machines used in the markets to provide a quality product to the Industry. Mr. Vaibhav Khalane has a target to manufacture superior quality jobs and provide them to the Industry.
